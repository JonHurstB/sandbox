ERIC

This week is ERIC week which stands for Education and Resources for Improving Childhood Continence. Most children wet the bed at one time or another but if you have any concerns or would like further information the number to call is 0845 3708008 or visit eric.org.uk. We will be having a service this week during school time to talk about staying healthy and drinking lots of water.
