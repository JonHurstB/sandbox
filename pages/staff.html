Parent Helpers

In addition, we encourage informal support from parent helpers. The activities these people support include reading, assistance during out of class visits, and extra-curricula activities of which a parent may have specialist knowledge. These have included:

  • Sewing
  • Bandaging
  • Computing (including Web page creation)

We are extremely grateful for the enthusiastic support we have had in this area. If you would be interested in offering some time, or have some specialist skill you would be prepared to come in and demonstrate to a class of eager pupils (and teachers!) then please do not hesitate to contact us.
