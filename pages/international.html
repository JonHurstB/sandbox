Links

We are developing a link with Woodford Primary School in Bergville, South Africa.

This year we are going to be sharing information about lives and traditions in each country to enable pupils to become more aware of other cultures.

Our first set of pictures show our Year 2 class and Class 2 at Woodford Primary. Our pupils have been fascinated to see similarities - school uniform and so many differences. You can see more photos comparing out schools and their surroundings here

Language Teaching

Language teaching has now started across the school with:

  • French in years 1, 2 and 6
