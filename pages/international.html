International Ties

Links

We are developing a link with Woodford Primary School in Bergville, South Africa.

This year we are going to be sharing information about lives and traditions in each country to enable pupils to become more aware of other cultures.

Our first set of pictures show our Year 2 class and Class 2 at Woodford Primary. Our pupils have been fascinated to see the many similarities and the many differences.

  • French in years 1, 2 and 6
