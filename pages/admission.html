Admission criteria

Places will be allocated according to these criteria (in priority order):

  1. parental wishes
  2. current family association (an elder sibling in the school at time of entry)
  3. health reasons (for which a medical certificate may be needed)
  4. proximity of children's homes and ease of access to school
