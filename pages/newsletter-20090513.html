Do you park considerately?

We have been approached by some of our neighbours about parents' parking. Please make sure you do not park in an inconsiderate manner or block anyone's driveway. Perhaps as the weather improves(!) you could think about parking in the Stag and Hounds car park and walking the rest of the way to school.

Parents' Questionnaire

We have had a very small number of questionnaires returned this year so I am working on the assumption that most people are happy!

An issue was raised by some parents about the use of inappropriate language. As a staff we always reprimand children we hear swearing - it is not acceptable in school! Please work with us to encourage your children to think carefully about the language that they use.
