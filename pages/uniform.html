Uniform & PE Clothes

We believe that wearing uniform can help foster a feeling of community and identity within our school. For this reason we ask you to send your child to school wearing clothes in these colours:

Boys' Uniform

  • White shirt or polo shirt
  • Grey trousers or shorts
  • Grey socks
  • Black shoes
  • Blue school sweatshirt

Girls' Uniform

  • White or yellow blouse or polo shirt
  • Grey pinafore dress, skirt or trousers
  • White socks
  • Grey tights
  • Black shoes
  • Blue school sweatshirt
  • Blue or yellow checked or striped dresses (in summer)

P.E

  • a pair of dark blue or black shorts
  • plimsolls (trainers may be worn outside only)
  • a plain white T-shirt

Jewellery

We prefer children not to wear jewellery to school at any time but if your child has pierced ears, studs are much safer. Large or dangly earrings are not permitted.
