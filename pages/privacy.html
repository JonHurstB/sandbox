Privacy Policy

Introduction

This page details our use of personal information that we collect when you use this Web site.

Use of Information

When you supply any personal information to us using the web site at churchill-pri.n-somerset.sch.uk (e.g. In response to a survey, leaving a message on our guest pages, or entering details into an online form) we have legal obligations towards you in the way we use those data. We must collect the information fairly, that is, we must explain how we will use it and we must tell you if we want to pass the information on to anyone else.

In general, any information you provide to us via this web site will only be used within the school and by its agents and service providers. Your information will be disclosed where we are obliged or permitted by law. Also, if you post or send offensive, inappropriate or objectionable content anywhere on or to our web site, we can use whatever information that is available to us about you to stop such behaviour. This may involve informing relevant third parties such as your school, employer, e-mail/Internet provider and law enforcement agencies about the content.

Any personal information that you provide to us using this web site will only be stored on our systems for as long as you use the service you have requested, and remove it in the event that the purpose has been met.
